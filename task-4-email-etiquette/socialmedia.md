Part B: Social Media Checklist & Analysis

Social Media Do’s and Don’tsDo's 

(Responsible Use)                                                                                    Don'ts (To Avoid)

1. Use a professional profile picture and bio for LinkedIn.                                       1. Don't post confidential or sensitive information.
2. Fact-check news or information before sharing.                                                 2. Don't engage in or encourage cyberbullying/trolling.
3. Set your personal accounts to 'Private' to protect data.                                       3. Don't post content that reflects poorly on your college.
4. Credit the original creator when sharing content.                                              4. Don't click on suspicious links or "clickbait."
5. Think twice about the long-term impact of a post.                                              5. Don't use unprofessional language in public comments.
