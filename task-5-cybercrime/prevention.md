Stay Safe Online: A Guide for Students

When you are using your account online never enter your UPI PIN to receive money. You only need a UPI PIN to send money or deduct money from your account.

Before you send money to someone verify their UPI IDs. Always double-check the name that is displayed on the screen before you hit the pay button.

Use locks or app locks. Make sure your payment apps, like GPay or PhonePe or Paytm require a fingerprint or a separate pattern to open them.

Enable two factor authentication. Use two factor authentication on your college email and all your social media accounts.

Be careful with emails that say they are urgent. If you get an email that asks you to reset your password or give your bank details treat it as suspicious.

When you are using the internet use Wi-Fi. Do not do banking transactions on public Wi-Fi, like the Wi-Fi at cafes or stations. Use your data instead.

When you are entering your details on a website check for https://. Only enter your details on websites that have a padlock icon and a secure URL.

Keep your phones software updated. Regularly update your phones operating system to protect against the security problems.

🚨 How to Report:

Portal: cybercrime.gov.in

Helpline: If you are a victim of fraud call 1930 immediately.
