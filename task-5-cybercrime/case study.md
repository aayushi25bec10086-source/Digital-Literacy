Scenario: The Reverse QR Code UPI Scam

What it's This is a trick where people get scammed while using UPI or online payment. The scammer makes the victim think they are getting money. 
Actually they end up sending it.

Step-by-Step Process:

* A student puts up an ad for something they want to sell like a book or lab coat on a website like OLX or a college WhatsApp group.

* The scammer contacts the student saying they want to buy it away. This makes the student trust them.

* The scammer then tells the student "I am sending you a QR code. Just scan it to get the payment."

* The student scans the code. Is asked to enter their UPI PIN. They think they are getting money so they enter the PIN without checking the screen

* The QR code was actually a "Collect Request". When the student enters their PIN it allows the scammer to take money from their account. The money is transferred immediately.

Target Audience: This scam targets college students and young people who often use UPI for transactions and selling things they no longer need.

Consequences: The student loses money away which can be very stressful for someone on a tight budget. 
It also makes them feel less confident, in using tools and can cause a lot of emotional stress from being tricked.
